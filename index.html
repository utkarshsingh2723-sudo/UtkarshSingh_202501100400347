Travel-Related Terms

Ecotourism
Responsible travel to natural areas that conserves the environment and improves the well-being of local people.
Layover
A period of time spent between flights, often in a different city, during a long journey.
Backpacking
A form of low-cost, independent travel often involving a backpack and staying in budget accommodations.
